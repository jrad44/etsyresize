Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Files

Uploaded images are processed in memory and never stored on our servers. Once processing is complete, the files are discarded and not retained.

Payment Information

Payments are handled securely by Stripe. We do not collect or store your credit card details. We only receive a transaction reference from Stripe to unlock your account.

Cookies

We use a small token saved in your browser’s localStorage to remember your purchase and remove the watermark. We do not use tracking cookies.

Ads

This site displays advertising via Google AdSense. Google may collect data to personalize ads based on your activity.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at support@etsyresize.com.
